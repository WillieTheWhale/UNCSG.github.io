A Message From Our President

Building a Better Carolina, Together

Adolfo Alvarez

105th Student Body President

As your 105th Student Body President and the first Latino to hold this position in UNC's history, I am deeply honored to serve you. Our administration is built on three foundational pillars: accessibility, affordability, and advocacy.

Together with Vice President Katherine Fiore and Chief of Cabinet Pranav Nemani, we are working tirelessly to address the challenges you face. From launching the Carolina Closet to securing 24-hour library access during finals, we're turning promises into progress.

Let's get to work - because every student deserves the resources and support to succeed at Carolina.
