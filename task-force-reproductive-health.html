Reproductive Health Task Force

Ensuring comprehensive access to reproductive health resources and care

Nayan Bala

Director, Reproductive Health Task Force

Nayan Bala is a junior majoring in Public Policy and minoring in Data Science and Health and Society. She enjoys her role because she is excited to see a future where no student will have to miss class because of their menstrual cycle, and a future where reproductive health is no longer a taboo topic. In her free time, she enjoys tennis and racket sports, and she is on the club squash team at UNC.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

The Reproductive Health Task Force works to ensure all students have access to comprehensive reproductive health resources, education, and care. We advocate for policies that support student health and work to break down stigma surrounding reproductive health topics.

Our Work

Key Priorities

Menstrual Products

Expanding access to menstrual products across campus buildings and supporting students experiencing period poverty.

Health Education

Improving reproductive health education and resources available to all students.

Family Planning

Reducing barriers to contraception and family planning services through advocacy and awareness.

Healthcare Coverage

Advocating for comprehensive healthcare coverage that meets student reproductive health needs.
