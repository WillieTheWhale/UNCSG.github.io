Office of the Secretary

Managing student government operations and maintaining official records

Devin Duncan

Secretary of the Undergraduate Student Body

Devin Duncan serves as the Secretary of the Undergraduate Student Body for the 2025-2026 academic year. As Secretary, Devin maintains accurate records of all student government proceedings, manages official documentation, and ensures transparency in organizational activities. Devin works closely with all branches of student government to facilitate effective communication and record-keeping across the executive branch, student congress, and honor court.

Our Work

Key Responsibilities

Meeting Minutes

Record and maintain minutes from all official student government meetings, ensuring accurate documentation of decisions and discussions.

Document Management

Organize and archive all official student government documents and records for current and future reference.

Communication

Facilitate information flow between student government branches and the student body through clear, consistent communication.

Transparency

Ensure all records are accessible to students per public records policies, maintaining openness in government operations.

Learn More

Office Operations

The Secretary's Office maintains comprehensive records of all student government activities. This includes meeting minutes, legislation, financial reports, and correspondence. The office works to ensure transparency and accountability in all student government operations.

The Secretary also supports the student senate by maintaining legislative records and tracking bills through the legislative process, ensuring all documentation is accurate and up-to-date.
