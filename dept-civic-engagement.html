Our Purpose

Our Mission

The Department of Civic Engagement is here to connect students with the issues, decisions, and communities shaping life at Carolina. Our goal is simple: make it easier for students to stay informed and take part in the conversations that matter.

At the campus level, we help facilitate engagement between students, the Alvarez Administration, the broader UNC administration, and the Town of Chapel Hill. Students at Carolina have never shied away from being engaged. Our work is to carry that energy forward.

Our Work

Current Projects

Cross-Departmental Newsletter

Keeping students informed about student government initiatives and campus issues through regular communications.

Election Participation

Working to increase voter turnout and engagement in student government elections across campus.

Town Partnership

Bringing civic engagement programming directly to students through partnership with the Town of Chapel Hill.

Admin-Student Dinners

Creating opportunities for direct dialogue between students and administrators through organized dinner programs.
