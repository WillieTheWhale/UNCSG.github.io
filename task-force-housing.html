Housing Task Force

Advancing housing affordability and accessibility for all students

Addison Truzy

Director, Housing Task Force

Addison Truzy is a junior majoring in Political Science and Advertising Public Relations with a minor in African American and Diaspora Studies. She enjoys her role because housing has had a profound impact on her experience at UNC, and she is eager to leverage her passion to create positive change and enhance student experiences on campus. She loves to dance in her free time. She has been dancing for 16 years, and on campus, she dances on the Kazi dance team as well as teaches hip-hop cardio dance classes.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

The Housing Task Force works to address critical challenges facing UNC students in securing affordable, accessible, and quality housing. Our initiatives focus on expanding on-campus housing options, improving off-campus rental conditions, and advocating for policies that protect student housing rights.

Our Work

Key Priorities

On-Campus Housing

Expanding affordable on-campus housing availability and improving housing quality and maintenance standards.

Off-Campus Support

Supporting students navigating off-campus rental markets with resources, guides, and advocacy.

Tenant Protections

Advocating for tenant protections and fair housing practices to protect student rights.

Housing Insecurity

Developing resources and support systems for students facing housing insecurity.
