Our Vision

Our Vision for Carolina

The Alvarez-Fiore Administration was built on three foundational pillars: accessibility, affordability, and advocacy. This platform represents our commitment to transparent, student-centered leadership that addresses the real challenges facing undergraduate students at Carolina.

From expanding basic needs resources to strengthening academic support systems, every initiative outlined in this platform stems from direct conversations with students across campus. Our administration is dedicated to removing barriers, creating opportunities, and ensuring every student has the resources to thrive.

Focus Areas

Strategic Priorities

Six focus areas driving our administration's work and impact

01

Basic Needs Security

Ensuring every student has access to essential resources including food, housing, professional attire, and emergency support through expanded programs like the Carolina Closet and enhanced food security initiatives.

Carolina Closet Food Security Housing Support
02

Academic Excellence

Strengthening academic support systems through extended library hours, improved study spaces, enhanced tutoring services, and advocating for smaller class sizes and more accessible faculty engagement.

24-Hour Access Study Spaces Tutoring
03

Mental Health Support

Expanding access to counseling services, reducing wait times, promoting comprehensive wellness resources, and destigmatizing mental health support across campus through proactive programming.

Counseling Access Wellness Programs Crisis Resources
04

Campus Safety

Creating a secure environment through improved lighting, expanded late-night transportation, enhanced safety resources, and transparent communication about security concerns and responses.

Lighting Transportation Security
05

Diversity & Inclusion

Building an equitable campus where all students feel welcome, supported, and empowered to succeed regardless of background, identity, or circumstance through inclusive policies and programs.

Equity Inclusion Support
06

Student Organizations

Supporting the heart of campus life by streamlining funding processes, providing organizational resources, removing administrative barriers, and empowering groups to thrive and serve students.

Funding Resources Support

Transparency

Accountability & Transparency

Our administration is committed to transparent governance and measurable outcomes. We track progress on every platform commitment through regular updates, public reports, and ongoing dialogue with the student body.

Every student has the right to know how their student government works on their behalf. We publish quarterly progress reports detailing funding allocations, policy implementations, and initiative outcomes.
