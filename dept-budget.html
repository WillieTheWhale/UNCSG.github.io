Our Purpose

Our Mission

The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) ensures financial operations of student government are carried out with transparency, efficiency, and accountability. Our mission is to strengthen the foundation of student government by responsibly managing its budget, monitoring expenditures, and ensuring resources are used to maximize the impact of initiatives serving the student body.

By combining careful oversight with long-term planning, we help student government achieve its goals while maintaining fiscal sustainability.

Our Work

What We're Working On

Reviewing Funding Requests

Evaluating requests from within the executive branch, ensuring resources are allocated strategically to maximize student impact.

Advising Departments

Helping departments implement their initiatives responsibly with sound financial planning and budget management.

Financial Reporting

Creating clearer financial reporting structures so the student body better understands how student government funds are invested.

Cross-Department Collaboration

Working with other cabinet departments to align financial management with their projects and strategic goals.
