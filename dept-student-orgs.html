Task Force

Student Organizations Task Force

Operation Connect: Facilitating collaboration between student organizations and strengthening their representation

Samantha Greene

Executive Director, Student Organizations Task Force

The Student Organizations Task Force, also known as Operation Connect, aims to facilitate collaboration between the diverse range of student organizations on campus. The task force also hopes to better represent these organizations in Student Government.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

Operation Connect serves as the bridge between student organizations and Student Government. We work to ensure that the voices and needs of over 900 registered student organizations are represented in campus decision-making processes.

Through comprehensive funding support, leadership development, and advocacy channels, we empower student organizations to thrive and serve their communities effectively.

Our Work

Key Initiatives

Comprehensive Funding Support

Supporting over 900 registered student organizations through $1.4 million annual appropriations and budgetary processes.

Student Organization Board Creation

Implementing Student Organization Board of Representatives creating direct communication channels between organizations and administration under Operation CONNECT.

Registration and Compliance

Administering fall 2025 registration requirements, treasurer certification testing, and compliance guidance through Carolina Union systems.

Leadership Development

Facilitating organizational development through funding hearings, leadership training support, and enhanced student government partnership initiatives.
