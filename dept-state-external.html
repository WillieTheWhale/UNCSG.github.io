Connecting student government with elected officials and ensuring student voices are heard in government

Contact: prasidha@unc.edu

Our Purpose

Our Mission

The Department of State and External Affairs aims to connect student government with those in elected office. This means working with the local, state, and federal government to ensure the voices of Chapel Hill students are heard and represented in government.

We build relationships with legislators, advocate for student priorities, and coordinate with other UNC System institutions on shared concerns.

Our Work

Current Initiatives

Legislative Engagement

Meeting with administrators about upcoming legislation and their impacts on students to ensure informed advocacy.

Student Representation

Meeting with legislators and representatives to ensure the voices of students are heard on issues affecting higher education.

UNC System Council

Serving as Vice Chair of UNC System Council of Student Body Presidents representing all 17 institutions across North Carolina.

Graduate Student Coalition

Coordinating North Carolina Graduate Student Coalition advocacy efforts on state-level policy with General Assembly representatives.
