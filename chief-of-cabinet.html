About the Cabinet

The Chief of Cabinet coordinates all cabinet departments and ensures effective implementation of the administration's policy platform. The cabinet meets weekly on Wednesdays at 5:06 PM in Carolina Union Suite 3109 to coordinate initiatives across 16 departments.

The cabinet is responsible for executing the Tar Heel Targets policy agenda, which focuses on basic needs, housing, reproductive health, STEM representation, and other key student priorities.
