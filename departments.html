How Our Departments Work

Each department focuses on specific areas of student life and campus improvement. Directors lead teams of dedicated students working on initiatives, events, and advocacy campaigns.

Departments collaborate across student government and with campus partners to maximize impact. All departments report to the Chief of the Cabinet and work closely with the Executive Council.

Students interested in joining a department should reach out to the department director or attend department meetings. All departments welcome new members who want to make a difference.
