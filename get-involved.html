Make an Impact

Why Get Involved?

Student Government offers a unique opportunity to shape the Carolina experience for over 20,000 undergraduate students. Whether you want to advocate for policy changes, plan events, or work on specific initiatives, there's a place for you.

Benefits of involvement include:

  • Develop leadership and professional skills
  • Network with administrators, faculty, and fellow students
  • Make tangible improvements to campus life
  • Gain experience in advocacy, communications, and project management
  • Build lasting friendships with passionate peers

No prior experience is required. We welcome all students who want to make a difference, regardless of major, year, or background.
