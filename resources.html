Support Services

Student Resources

Comprehensive resources and support services for UNC undergraduate students

The UNC Student Government is committed to connecting students with the resources and support services they need to succeed at Carolina. This page provides direct access to essential campus resources, from housing assistance to academic support.

Housing

Housing Resources

Support for students navigating on-campus and off-campus housing options, tenant rights, and housing assistance programs.

Off-Campus Housing Guide

Official UNC Off-Campus Student Life guide with information on move-in and move-out, safety and security, party registration, and student-friendly apartment listings for 2025-2026.

Visit Resource

Tenant Rights Information

Comprehensive information on tenant rights and responsibilities under NC Statutes and Fair Housing Act, including the Declaration of Tenants Rights and Obligations.

Visit Resource

Housing Assistance Programs

Detailed guide covering budgeting, lease considerations, utilities, and financial planning for students moving to off-campus housing.

Visit Resource

Carolina Student Legal Services

Free legal advice and representation for landlord-tenant matters, lease reviews, security deposits, and evictions. Contact: (919) 962-1303 or csls@unc.edu.

Visit Resource

Learn more about the Housing Task Force

STEM

STEM Resources

Career services, research opportunities, laboratory access, and student organizations for students in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics programs.

STEM Career Services

UNC Career Center resources for STEM students including Handshake access, career fairs, workshops, and specialized tools like MyIDP for graduate students.

Visit Resource

Research Opportunities

Office for Undergraduate Research maintains a searchable database of research opportunities, administers Summer Undergraduate Research Fellowships, and provides funding across all disciplines.

Visit Resource

Laboratory Access Information

UNC Chemistry Department's CRITICL page provides information about state-of-the-art instrumentation training and access for undergraduates, graduate students, and researchers.

Visit Resource

STEM Student Organizations

Heel Life is UNC's official student organization portal with over 900 registered organizations, including numerous STEM clubs and upcoming events.

Visit Resource

Learn more about the STEM Task Force

Health

Reproductive Health Resources

Comprehensive sexual and reproductive health services, education, and resources for all students.

Campus Health Sexual Health Services

Comprehensive sexual and reproductive health resources, including contraception, STI screening, gynecology services, and pregnancy services. Appointments: (919) 966-2281.

Visit Resource

Menstrual Product Locations

Healthy Heels To Go vending machines at Carolina Union, Rams Head Recreation Center, and Gillings School of Public Health offer over-the-counter health items including menstrual products.

Visit Resource

SHARE: Sexual Health Education

Sexual Health and Relationship Education provides education, one-on-one meetings with trained educators, group outreach programs, and free safer sex supplies. Contact: (919) 962-9355.

Visit Resource

Emergency Contraception

Information on emergency contraception access at Campus Health Pharmacy, Student Stores Pharmacy, and Healthy Heels To Go vending machines without appointment or prescription.

Visit Resource

Learn more about the Reproductive Health Task Force

Visit Resource

University Libraries

Research assistance, study spaces, technology resources, and extensive collections to support your academic work.

Visit Resource

Academic Advising

Professional academic advisors help with course selection, degree planning, academic policies, and connecting to campus resources.

Visit Resource

Physical health, mental health, and wellness services to support your overall wellbeing.

Campus Health Services

Primary care, urgent care, pharmacy, laboratory, radiology, physical therapy, immunizations, and specialty services for students.

Visit Resource

Counseling and Psychological Services

CAPS provides confidential mental health services including counseling, psychiatry, crisis intervention, and consultation. 24/7 crisis support available.

Visit Resource

Student Wellness

Health promotion programs, peer education, wellness coaching, and resources on topics including stress management, sleep, nutrition, and healthy relationships.

Visit Resource

Campus Recreation

Fitness facilities, group exercise classes, intramural sports, outdoor adventures, and wellness programs to support physical health and social connection.

Carolina Cupboard

Campus food pantry providing free groceries and essential items to students experiencing food insecurity. Confidential and accessible to all students.

Visit Resource

Student Assistance Fund

Support for students navigating personal challenges, academic difficulties, or campus concerns. Advocates for student needs and connects students with resources.
