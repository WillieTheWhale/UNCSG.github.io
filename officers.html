About

The Executive Council

The Executive Council leads the Undergraduate Executive Branch of UNC Student Government. The Student Body President and Vice President are elected by the undergraduate student body, while other officers are appointed and confirmed through the student government process.

Together, the Executive Council works to advance student interests, manage student government operations, and advocate for the undergraduate student body with university administration and external stakeholders.
